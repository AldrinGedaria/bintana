Frequently Asked Question

got questions? we've got answers

Do you offer free on-site measurement?

+

Once you agree to the quotation, we’ll be happy to proceed with the actual measurement.

Can I get a free quote?

+

To get a free quote, you may send the details such as the quantity, dimensions, frame type, glass, and color, or simply send the schedule of windows and doors measurement to our socials, or email.

Free Delivery?

+

We offer free shipping on orders ₱100,000 or more within Luzon.

Where do you deliver and install?

+

Currently, we ship within Luzon, Philippines. If you are interested in placing an order outside of Luzon, please contact us at enriupvcglassandaluminum@gmail.com.

What warranty do you offer?

+

Our products are built to last. If one of your products has been damaged, please get in touch with us at hello@holcombstudio.com.
